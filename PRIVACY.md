# Privacy Policy — Dranyen

**Last updated: 26 June 2026**

Dranyen is published by the **Terma Heritage Foundation**. This policy explains
how the app handles your information. The short version: it doesn't collect any.

## We do not collect personal data

Dranyen has no accounts, no sign-in, no analytics, no advertising, and no
third-party trackers. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal
information about you.

## Microphone

The tuner uses your device's microphone to detect the pitch of the string you
play. This happens **live, entirely on your device**. Your audio is:

- never recorded or saved,
- never sent to us or to any third party,
- discarded the instant it has been analysed for pitch.

The microphone is used only while the tuner is actively listening, and only for
pitch detection.

## Works offline

Dranyen functions fully offline. It makes no network requests to us, and it
does not need an internet connection to tune or to read the Learn section.

## Children

The app is suitable for all ages and collects no data from anyone, including
children.

## Changes

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted at the policy
URL below with a new "last updated" date.
